What Is Coding? A Beginner's Guide

Published: 2026. 05. 25 | Category: Coding Basics

Many people imagine a screen full of numbers and English words when they hear the word coding. The core idea is much simpler: coding is a way to communicate with computers.

1. Coding is like learning a new language

Just as we learn English or Japanese to speak with people from other countries, we learn programming languages to communicate with computers. When we give clear instructions, the computer follows them.

2. Why should you learn coding?

Coding is not only for people who want to become developers. It helps you build logical thinking by breaking problems into smaller steps and solving them in order.
